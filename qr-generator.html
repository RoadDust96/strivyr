Security Notice

Only create QR codes for content you trust. This tool includes basic security checks, but always verify QR codes before sharing them with others.

Be cautious of: Shortened URLs, suspicious downloads, password requests, or offers that seem too good to be true.

Usage Tips

  • URLs should include http:// or https://
  • Higher error correction helps with damaged codes
  • Use larger sizes for long text or URLs
  • Test your QR codes with your phone's camera
