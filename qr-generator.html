Security Notice:

Only create QR codes for content you trust. This tool includes basic security checks, but always verify QR codes before sharing them with others. Be cautious of:

  • Shortened URLs that hide the real destination
  • Suspicious file downloads or executable links
  • Requests for personal information or passwords
  • Links that seem too good to be true

Tips:

  • URLs should include http:// or https://
  • Higher error correction helps QR codes work even if slightly damaged or dirty
  • Use larger sizes for long text or URLs to make them easier to scan
  • Test your QR codes with your phone's camera to make sure they work
